Education

2001 - B.S. in Geochemistry, Univ. of Science and Technology of China, China

Research Interests

Study of the composition and evolution of the continental crust, mantle heterogeneity and the origin and evolution of the solar system using stable isotopes measured by multi-collector inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry (MC-ICPMS).
